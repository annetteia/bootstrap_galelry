Black Hole

With a strong gravitational field, black holes are able to pull space around them while generating more energy than our sun. A supermassive black hole, like the one pictured above, is likely at the center of our galaxy, named Sagittarius A.

Andromeda Galaxy

A spiral galaxy hurling in our direction, Andromeda is expected to collide with the Milkyway Galaxy in approximately 4 billion years. It can be seen with the naked eye in the night sky.

Crab Nebula

Nebulae are clouds of plasma, dust, and gasses that when compressed, ignite the hydrogen present in them forming new stars. They can also consist of the remnants of an exploded star, like the Crab Nebula pictured above.

Frescoes like these were found at the Bonampak Mayan Archaeological Site. CONSERVATION

Whale Shark

Commercial fishing and microplastic pollution have caused this docile shark specied to become endangered. They can be observed up close in Mexico, The Philippines, Thailand, and many other warm countries.

Last updated 3 mins ago

Bonobo

One of our closest living relatives, the bonobo is currently endangered, with their biggest threats being hunters and poachers. This non-violent primate works in unison with it's family, proving to be highly intelligent and social. They can only be found in the Democratic Republic of the Congo.

Last updated 3 mins ago

Resplendent Quetzal

Considered sacred to the ancient mesoamerican people, this rare bird's feathers were worn during ceremonies by both Aztec and Mayan Priests. Today, they're threatened by deforestation.
